# Andrej Karpathy

AI researcher and educator. Former director of AI at Tesla, former researcher at OpenAI. Known for making AI concepts accessible through writing and teaching.

## In This Knowledge Base

- Authored the [[LLM - Karpathy LLM Wiki|LLM Wiki]] pattern — a methodology for building LLM-maintained personal knowledge bases as an alternative to RAG.
- Uses Obsidian as the browsing interface alongside an LLM agent, describing the workflow as: "Obsidian is the IDE; the LLM is the programmer; the wiki is the codebase."
- Advocates for personal, curated knowledge over generic retrieval — connects the idea to Vannevar Bush's [[Knowledge Management - Memex|Memex]] (1945).
