# XR Venues

> Tier-0 venues, labs, and publication channels for XR development and the adjacent fields that feed into it — Graphics, HCI, Machine Learning, Computer Vision, LLMs, Games — across Western and Chinese research ecosystems. Institutional, peer-reviewed, or authoritative industry-vendor sources only. No personal blogs, newsletters, or community aggregators.
>
> URLs are canonical; months indicate typical calendar slot.

---

## Computer Graphics

### Conferences

| Venue | Known for | Month | URL |
|---|---|---|---|
| **SIGGRAPH** | The premier graphics venue. Full papers → ACM TOG. Co-located **Courses** (e.g. "Advances in Real-Time Rendering in Games"), **Talks**, and **Real-Time Live!** are first-tier in their own right. | August | siggraph.org |
| **SIGGRAPH Asia** | SIGGRAPH's Asian sibling; same rigor, often heavier on geometry processing and CG/VFX applications. | December | asia.siggraph.org |
| **Eurographics (EG)** | European flagship; full papers → CGF. Strong on geometry, simulation, rendering theory. | April–May | eg.org |
| **HPG (High-Performance Graphics)** | Small, laser-focused on real-time rendering pipelines, GPU architecture, ray-tracing engineering. | July | highperformancegraphics.org |
| **I3D (Symposium on Interactive 3D Graphics and Games)** | Real-time rendering, game-engine techniques, spatial data structures. Papers → ACM PACMCGIT. | May | i3dsymposium.org |
| **EGSR (Eurographics Symposium on Rendering)** | More rigorous than SIGGRAPH specifically on rendering (ray tracing, GI, BRDF theory). | June–July | egsr.org |
| **SCA (Symposium on Computer Animation)** | Character animation, physics simulation, skinning. | August | scasymposium.org |
| **SGP (Symposium on Geometry Processing)** | Meshing, parameterization, SDFs, point clouds. | July | geometryprocessing.org |
| **Pacific Graphics** | Asia-Pacific regional; papers → CGF. Strong on image synthesis and neural rendering. | October | |

## XR / VR / AR / HCI

### Conferences

| Venue | Known for | Month | URL |
|---|---|---|---|
| **IEEE VR** | The academic VR conference; absorbed IEEE 3DUI as a track. | March | ieeevr.org |
| **IEEE ISMAR** | Mixed/augmented reality: tracking, SLAM, displays, passthrough. | October | ismar.net |
| **ACM UIST** | HCI systems; many XR interaction/input papers. Small, selective. | October | uist.acm.org |
| **ACM CHI** | The broader HCI flagship; XR is a strong sub-track. | April–May | chi.acm.org |
| **ACM VRST** | VR software and technology — more systemsy than IEEE VR. | October–November | vrst.acm.org |

### Journals

| Venue | Known for |
|---|---|
| **IEEE TVCG** | Large portion of VR/AR archival work lands here. |

### Industry research labs

- **Meta Reality Labs Research** (research.facebook.com/teams/reality-labs) — *the* largest XR research org. Displays, tracking, hand/eye, avatars, haptics, AppSW/reprojection (cf. [[XR - Space-Warp]]).
- **Google AR / Google Research** — ARCore, SLAM, Geospatial, depth sensing.
- **Apple Machine Learning Research** (machinelearning.apple.com) — Vision Pro avatars, eye tracking. Low volume, high impact.
- **Microsoft Research (HoloLens team)** — scene understanding, occlusion, MR, Azure Spatial Anchors.
- **Snap Research** — AR on phones, neural filters, body tracking.

### Standards bodies and official specs

- **Khronos Group** (khronos.org) — **OpenXR**, glTF, Vulkan. Spec repos on GitHub are the source of truth for cross-platform XR.
- **W3C Immersive Web Working Group** — **WebXR** specification.
- **ISO/IEC JTC 1/SC 29** — media standards overlap with XR streaming (video, point cloud compression).

## Chinese research ecosystem

Significant volume of graphics, CV, ML, and XR work now originates from mainland Chinese institutions, much of it appearing first on arXiv and often at Chinese venues less visible to English-speaking readers.

### Top academic institutions

- **Tsinghua University (THU)** — ML/CV/graphics; generative models, neural rendering, robotics.
- **Peking University (PKU)** — NLP/LLM, systems, broad CS.
- **Shanghai AI Laboratory (SHLAB)** (shlab.org.cn) — national lab; InternLM, InternVL, embodied AI.
- **Zhejiang University (ZJU)** — graphics + animation; CAD/CG lab.
- **Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)** — CV, robotics.
- **Chinese Academy of Sciences — Institute of Computing Technology (CAS ICT)** and **Institute of Automation (CASIA)** — national labs.
- **University of Science and Technology of China (USTC)** — graphics, CV, signal processing.
- **Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST)** — CV/ML.
- **Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK)** — MMLab is among the most prolific CV labs globally; SenseTime roots.
- **University of Hong Kong (HKU)** — ML, 3D vision, robotics.

### Top industrial labs

- **ByteDance Research** (bytedance.com/en/research) — video understanding, Seed/Doubao foundation models; PICO VR research feeds here.
- **Tencent AI Lab** (ai.tencent.com) — NLP, CV, RL, game AI.
- **Alibaba DAMO Academy** (damo.alibaba.com) — Qwen foundation models, CV, autonomous driving.
- **Baidu Research** (research.baidu.com) — Ernie model family, Apollo, speech.
- **Huawei Noah's Ark Lab** (noahlab.com.hk) — NLP, CV, recommender systems; publishes at top venues.
- **Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA)** — Beijing; graphics, CV, NLP. Tier-0 regardless of geography.
- **SenseTime Research** (sensetime.com) — CV-first, broad now.
- **DeepSeek** — open-weight LLM lab; high-visibility since 2024.
- **Zhipu AI (智谱)** — GLM model family; open-weight releases.

## Scholarly infrastructure

Institutional tools where tier-0 venue output lands or is made navigable.

- **arXiv** (arxiv.org) — primary preprint server. Relevant sub-categories: `cs.GR`, `cs.CV`, `cs.CL`, `cs.LG`, `cs.AI`, `cs.HC`.
- **OpenReview** (openreview.net) — hosts ICLR, NeurIPS, TMLR with full public reviewer discussion.
- **Semantic Scholar** (semanticscholar.org) — Allen Institute citation-graph tool.
- **Papers With Code** (paperswithcode.com) — pairs papers with implementations.
- **DBLP** (dblp.org) — cleanest bibliographic metadata source.
- **ACM Digital Library** (dl.acm.org) — SIGGRAPH/CHI/UIST full-text.
- **IEEE Xplore** (ieeexplore.ieee.org) — IEEE VR, ISMAR, TVCG, TPAMI.
