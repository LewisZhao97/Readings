## Candidates

### 1. GRTX: Efficient Ray Tracing for 3D Gaussian-Based Rendering

- **Authors:** Junseo Lee, Sangyun Jeon, Jungi Lee, Junyong Park, Jaewoong Sim
- **Venue / Date:** arXiv (cs.GR + cs.AR) — submitted 2026-01-28
- **URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.20429
- **DOI:** 10.48550/arXiv.2601.20429
- **Hook:** Hardware + software co-design for Gaussian ray tracing — streamlined acceleration structures, dedicated traversal hardware, and per-ray checkpointed resume to avoid BVH root restarts.
- **Why-matched:** Most recent on-topic paper returned (Jan 2026); sits exactly at the XR-ray-tracing × Gaussian-splatting intersection with a systems angle that matters for HMD-class compute budgets.
- **Status:** new

### 2. Hardware-Rasterized Ray-Based Gaussian Splatting (VKRayGS)

- **Authors:** Samuel Rota Bulò, Nemanja Bartolovic, Lorenzo Porzi, Peter Kontschieder (Meta Reality Labs, Zürich)
- **Venue / Date:** CVPR 2025 (IEEE/CVF), pp. 485–494; arXiv 2503.18682 (v2 Jun 2025)
- **URL:** https://facebookresearch.github.io/vkraygs/ · https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2025/html/Bulo_Hardware-Rasterized_Ray-Based_Gaussian_Splatting_CVPR_2025_paper.html
- **DOI:** 10.48550/arXiv.2503.18682 (CVPR proceedings DOI via IEEE not yet indexed)
- **Hook:** First rasterizer-based pipeline for ray-based 3DGS (RayGS) that hits VR/MR-grade frame rates; adds alias-free MIP handling.
- **Why-matched:** Meta Reality Labs explicitly targets VR/MR quality bar; "first enabling RayGS at frame rates sufficient for quality-sensitive XR" is the headline claim. Strong venue + industry signal.
- **Status:** new

### 3. VR-Splatting: Foveated Radiance Field Rendering via 3D Gaussian Splatting and Neural Points

- **Authors:** Linus Franke, Laura Fink, Marc Stamminger (FAU Erlangen-Nürnberg)
- **Venue / Date:** Proc. ACM Comput. Graph. Interact. Tech. (PACMCGIT) Vol 8 No 1, article 18 — May 2025 (I3D 2025 track)
- **URL:** https://arxiv.org/html/2410.17932v2 · https://lfranke.github.io/vr_splatting
- **DOI:** 10.1145/3728302
- **Hook:** Hybrid foveated pipeline — 3DGS in the periphery, neural points in the fovea, driven by HMD eye-tracking; 90Hz-capable on VR.
- **Why-matched:** Purpose-built for VR at perceptual thresholds; directly bridges two hot threads (radiance fields + foveated rendering) for XR.
- **Status:** new

### 4. Radiant Foam: Real-Time Differentiable Ray Tracing

- **Authors:** Shrisudhan Govindarajan, Daniel Rebain, Kwang Moo Yi, Andrea Tagliasacchi
- **Venue / Date:** arXiv (cs.CV) — 2025-02-03
- **URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.01157
- **DOI:** 10.48550/arXiv.2502.01157
- **Hook:** Volumetric-mesh ray tracing of a learned scene representation — matches 3DGS speed/quality *without* rasterization constraints and without hardware RT acceleration (no RTX/HWRT required).
- **Why-matched:** Proposes an alternative to rasterized radiance-field rendering; runs on commodity programmable GPUs, which is the realistic compute envelope for stand-alone XR headsets.
- **Status:** new

### 5. MetaSapiens: Real-Time Neural Rendering with Efficiency-Aware Pruning and Accelerated Foveated Rendering

- **Authors:** Weikai Lin, Yu Feng, Yuhao Zhu (University of Rochester)
- **Venue / Date:** ASPLOS '25 — Rotterdam, Mar–Apr 2025
- **URL:** https://arxiv.org/html/2407.00435v3 · https://horizon-lab.org/metasapiens/
- **DOI:** 10.1145/3669940.3707227
- **Hook:** First real-time point-based neural renderer on mobile-class GPUs (pruning + the first foveated-rendering technique for PBNR).
- **Why-matched:** Tackles the "3–4% of desktop-GPU compute" regime that Quest/Snapdragon-XR2 class devices live in; architectural approach, not just an algorithm.
- **Status:** new
