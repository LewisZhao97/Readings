## Candidates

### 1. Real-time Rendering with a Neural Irradiance Volume (NIV)
- **Authors:** Arno Coomans, Giacomo Nazzaro, Edoardo A. Dominici, Christian Döring, et al.
- **Venue/Date:** arXiv preprint, 13 Feb 2026
- **URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.12949
- **DOI:** 10.48550/arXiv.2602.12949
- **Hook:** Neural replacement for probe-based irradiance volumes — real-time diffuse GI with low memory, targeted at game engines. Supports moving objects and optional parametric (non-static) irradiance fields.
- **Why matched:** Direct "real-time GI for games" research; natural successor to probe-GI pipelines common in engines.
- **Status:** new

### 2. Forget Superresolution, Sample Adaptively (when Path Tracing)
- **Authors:** Martin Bálint, Corentin Salaün, Hans-Peter Seidel, Karol Myszkowski
- **Venue/Date:** arXiv preprint, 9 Feb 2026 (MPI Informatik)
- **URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.08642
- **DOI:** 10.48550/arXiv.2602.08642
- **Hook:** End-to-end adaptive sampling + denoising pipeline for the sub-1-spp regime, arguing adaptive allocation beats uniform-sparse + upscale. Includes a gather-based pyramidal denoiser and learnable albedo demodulation.
- **Why matched:** Frontier of real-time path tracing reconstruction — complements the Mob-FGSR / neural-upscaling family already in the KB.
- **Status:** new

### 3. Real-time Neural Six-way Lightmaps

### 4. Streaming Real-Time Rendered Scenes as 3D Gaussians

- **DOI:** 10.48550/arXiv.2604.02851
